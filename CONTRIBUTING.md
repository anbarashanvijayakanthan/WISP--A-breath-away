# 🤝 Contributing to WISP

We are on a mission to reduce neonatal mortality, and we need your help! Whether you are a doctor, a coder, or a hardware geek, here is how you can contribute:

### 🛠️ How to Help
1. **AI Improvement:** Help us refine the 1D-CNN model in the `ml-model/` folder.
2. **Bug Fixes:** Found a glitch in the `mobile-app/`? Open an Issue!
3. **Documentation:** Help us translate the `README.md` into more languages.

### 🚀 Getting Started
1. **Fork** the repository.
2. Create a new **Branch** (`git checkout -b feature/YourFeature`).
3. **Commit** your changes.
4. Open a **Pull Request**.

*All contributors will be featured in our Hall of Fame!*
